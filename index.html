In loving memory and to honor Tiamiyu Adepoju Raji (aka “Ade”), a private charity has been set-up under section 501 (c) (3) of United States.

The Adepoju Raji Foundation

We encourage and nurture philanthropy in our family and we share our enthusiasm. We look for passion in our grantees. We expect to enjoy the work that we share..

DEDICATION

The development and success of this charity is dedicated to Alhaja Afusat Adunni Raji, Alhaja Sherifat Ronke Raji, Mrs. Oluremi Grace Raji, Mrs. Mojisola Abebi Raji, Mrs. Beatrice Bolawa Oyedokun and all descendant mothers of Elejin Family and friends.

MISSION

To partner with nonprofits to improve the quality of life for families and communities, in places where Elejin-Raji family members and friends live.

Facilitating job skills training opportunities that include the development of the entire individual whereby providing the trainees with a full set of skills for success.

VISION

To create strong and acceptable brand and value in communities where families thrive.

OUR VALUES

Respect and Transparency

We respect and value the expertise of those with whom we collaborate in our shared work: our non-profit partners, the individuals who lead them, each other as family members and staff.

Our decision making process of accomplishing our grant making process amongst and between us and our partners are transparent.

Support are only extended to value adding, desiring and deserving ventures.



Inclusion and Diversity

We value the diverse perspective of our partners, the people they serve and others who work in our areas of interest.

We invite and encourage broad participation of all interested friends and family members.

Effectiveness

We invest the Foundation’s resources in effective nonprofits that change lives. We evaluate all we do and make adjustments based on what we learn.

Skill Building and Shared Learning

We work in ways that emphasize learning, skill building and knowledge sharing among our family, friends, and our partners.
